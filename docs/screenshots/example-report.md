# Example Prospect Research Report

**Target URL:** https://www.cloudflare.com

**Cloudflare Website Audit Report**

**Company Overview**

Cloudflare is a cloud-based platform that provides a suite of services for building, securing, and scaling applications. They target businesses and organizations that need to manage their infrastructure, security, and performance. Cloudflare is a leader in the cybersecurity and networking industry, powering 42% of the Fortune 500 companies.

**Website Assessment**

* The website is modern and well-designed, with a clear and concise value proposition.
* The hero video is engaging, but it's currently set to Low mode, which limits its full-motion capabilities.
* The content is well-organized, and the use of short paragraphs and bullet points makes it easy to read.
* The website has a clear call-to-action (CTA) to start building for free, which encourages visitors to take action.
* The design is professional, and the use of white space and imagery makes the content easy to read.

**Conversion Leaks**

* The website lacks a clear and prominent trust signal, such as a security badge or a customer testimonial.
* The contact information is buried in the footer, making it difficult for visitors to find and contact the company.
* The website has a slow messaging system, as the "Start building" button is not prominent enough.
* There is no clear explanation of the benefits of using Cloudflare's services, which may confuse visitors.
* The website could benefit from more social proof, such as customer logos or reviews.

**Actionable Recommendations**

1. Add a clear and prominent trust signal to the website, such as a security badge or a customer testimonial.
2. Move the contact information to a more prominent location, such as the header or sidebar.
3. Make the "Start building" button more prominent and visible.
4. Add more social proof, such as customer logos or reviews.
5. Create a clear and concise explanation of the benefits of using Cloudflare's services.
6. Consider adding a free trial or demo to allow visitors to experience the platform firsthand.
7. Optimize the website for mobile devices, as many visitors will access the site on their smartphones.

**Competitive Notes**

Based on the website content, Cloudflare is positioning itself as a leader in the cybersecurity and networking industry. They are highlighting their ability to power 42% of the Fortune 500 companies and their expertise in building, securing, and scaling applications. Cloudflare is also emphasizing their focus on security, performance, and scalability, which are key differentiators in the market. However, the website could benefit from more specific examples and case studies to demonstrate the value of their services.
